Disclaimer

For RCAs and DFs: Please fill out all fields, including your full name.

For other community members: We believe in peer-to-peer accountability and in students taking responsibility for creating the type of community they want to live in.

With that in mind, any Yale-NUS community member can submit an Incident Statement. If you notice something that breaks the guidelines of the Yale-NUS community and/ or the Code of Student Conduct of Yale-NUS, please record a description of the incident here. These incidents could include noise complaints during quiet hours, witnessing smoking in the Sky Gardens or in suites, or any other incidents that oppose the tenants of a strong community and could possibly violate the Code of Student Conduct.

*Please note that incidents of sexual misconduct should be reported via the official reporting process noted here. All incidents that are logged here may be investigated by the Student Conduct Board and students may be charged with the potential violation of a Yale-NUS policy. If this happens, students will be notified that they will need to set up a meeting with the Conduct Board and will have an opportunity to discuss this incident.
